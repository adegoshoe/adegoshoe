About Adego Shoe Industry

At Adego, we blend craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver shoes that feel as good as they look. Our collections are designed for everyday life—whether you’re heading to the office, the gym, or out on the trail.

We source high-quality materials and rigorously test every pair to ensure longevity, comfort, and performance.

Our Factory in Bahadurgarh

Situated in Bahadurgarh, Haryana, our flagship facility powers Adego’s production with a blend of traditional shoemaking and modern engineering. Every pair is crafted with care to meet our standards for comfort, durability, and style.

From cutting and stitching to lasting and finishing, our integrated lines operate under strict quality controls—ensuring consistent results from premium materials to final packaging.

  • Advanced manufacturing technology for precise, efficient production
  • Skilled, experienced workforce trained in world-class shoemaking
  • Rigorous quality checks at every stage to deliver top-quality shoes
